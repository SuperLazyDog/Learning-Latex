簡単な数式2

\begin{equation}
\frac{\pi}{2}=
\left(\int_{k=0}^{\infty}   \frac{\sin x}{\sqrt x} dx       \right)^2 = 
\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{(2k)!}{2^{2k}(k!)^2}  \frac{1}{2k+1} =
\prod_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{4k^2}{4k^2-1}
\end{equation}
